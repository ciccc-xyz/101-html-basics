International Trade Agreement

Agreement Overview

Our company, XYZ Corporation, has entered into an international trade agreement with Company A based in Country B. The agreement outlines the terms and conditions for the import and export of goods between our two companies.

Terms and Conditions

  • XYZ Corporation agrees to export Product A to Company A.
  • Company A agrees to import Product A from XYZ Corporation.
  • The price for Product A will be determined by mutual agreement between the two companies.
  • Product A will be shipped from XYZ Corporation's facility in Country C.
  • The responsibility for shipping and handling of Product A will be determined by mutual agreement between the two companies.
  • The agreement will be valid for one year from the date of signing.
  • Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days' notice.
