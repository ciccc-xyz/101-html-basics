International Business Plan

Executive Summary

Our company, XYZ Corporation, plans to expand into the international market. Our goal is to increase our revenue by 50% within the next three years.

Market Analysis

Our target market is primarily young adults aged 18-35 who are interested in sustainable and ethical products. Our main competitors are companies A and B, who currently hold 60% of the market share.

Marketing Strategy

We plan to create a marketing campaign that targets young adults aged 18-35 through social media and influencer marketing. We will also emphasize the sustainable and ethical features of our products.

Financial Plan

We project that our international expansion will require an investment of $500,000 in the first year, with an expected revenue of $750,000. We plan to reinvest our profits into further expansion.
